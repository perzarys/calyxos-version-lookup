Lookup tool for recent and historical CalyxOS builds

This tool is written by me, perzarys, to allow for an easy retrieval of old builds for testing or development purpose.

Some notes before you start:

  • Not all links work.
  • Newest builds may not have been published yet, or are in testing/beta state.
  • Some builds may have been pulled due to problems.
  • You might not be able to flash old builds due to rollback protection.
  • Use this tool at your own risk.
